# Symmetry Groups of the Plane
This is a paper and accompanying talk slides I prepared for a course in Euclidean and non-Euclidean geometries. It briefly covers a fascinating intersection of geometry and algebra. This material is very light in complexity and requires little background knowledge beyond basic familiarity with geometric concepts like translation, rotation, and reflection.
